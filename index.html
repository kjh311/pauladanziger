Welcome to the Official Paula Danziger Website!

Paula Danziger, American children’s author, wrote more than 30 books, notably the popular Amber Brown series, that presented serious issues with humour and honesty. Danziger was also well known for her warm and flamboyant personal style. Her books, all of which remained in print, won numerous awards.

Paula wrote for over thirty years and would have happily continued writing. There have been over thirty publications, all of which have had impact on the most important part of society and its future - kids. Let us all celebrate, remember, and learn through her novels and series, addressing real life concerns of kids of all ages, which still hold true today.
